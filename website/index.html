Safe Deal
Let's make safer deals involving no middlemen.

Safe Deal is an online escrow service that works for buyers and sellers to make safe and trustworthy trade. The smart contract acts as a middle man, not physically available but as a program. The total payment to be made by the buyer and time required by the seller to deliver the product are given as the inputs. If the seller is able to make the delivery of product in the given time, the money will get transferred into his account from buyer's account automatically. Once the delivery is made or the timeline is achieved, contract will get deleted. Smart contract is an efficient, automated and money saving method by practically cutting the middle man from the trust equation. A smart contract is trustworthy as all the conditions of the deal and any changes to made in the deal are done only after the agreement from both the parties.

How It Works

Registration

To get on board, both the parties register.

Verification

Verification of public key using blockchain.

Analysis

The data is collected and analysed to check if the service was completed in the required manner.

Payment

Payment is done which was held by the contract.
